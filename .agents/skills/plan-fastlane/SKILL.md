# Plan Fastlane Compatibility Alias

Delegate to `$fastlane` and preserve the user's intake, Gate A, design, or Gate
B intent. Do not run a parallel planner, write independently, invoke a
challenger outside its defined condition, or approve a gate. The `fastlane`
coordinator loads `references/define.md` or `references/design.md` and remains
the only writer.
